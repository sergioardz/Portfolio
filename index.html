About Me

Hey there! My name is Sergio Rodriguez and I'm from Monterrey Mexico. I am a Quality and Continuous Improvement Professional with over 10 years of experience. I have worked for OEM's in Mexico, Germany and indirectly in the US with large multi-national Companies. This gave me the chance to work in Germany for 4 years as Project Leader for the Manufacturing International Operations Department in the HQ of the Low Voltage Products Division for Siemens AG in Regensburg, Bavaria. Quite a challenge and experience!

Having a strong deep insight about the current IT solutions for the manufacturing Industry in Mexico, specifically for small to medium size companies. And I am sure that sooner than later with this new set of coding skills I will be able to bring value to the Manufacturing Community in my beloved city and country.
