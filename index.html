Hey there! My name is Sergio Rodriguez and I'm from Monterrey Mexico. I am a Quality and Continuous Improvement Professional with over 10 years of experience and I also consider myself as a Technology enthusiast.

I have a bachelor’s in industrial engineering and a master’s in business administration, currently undergoing a Coding Bootcamp. Native Spanish speaker and fluent on both English and German. Six Sigma, Lean Manufacturing, Quality Systems and APQP are some of my acquired skills over the years

I have worked for large multi-national OEM Companies in Mexico and Germany. My professional development gave me the chance to work in Germany for 4 years as Project Leader for the Manufacturing International Operations Department at the HQ of the Low Voltage Products Division for Siemens AG in Regensburg, Bavaria. Quite a challenge and experience if I might add!

I am currently working on my new quest: to become a web developer. I am all about creating value and eliminating waste in whichever endeavor I am dealing with at the time. Let’s leverage today’s tech advantages and transform data into information on to well-based decision making. Let’s create better suited solutions and continue to create value.
